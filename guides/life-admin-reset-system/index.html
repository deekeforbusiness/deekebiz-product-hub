Life Admin Guide

How to get back on top of life admin when everything feels messy.

Life admin gets heavy when bills, paperwork, appointments, renewals, calls, and loose tasks all compete for attention. The first goal is not perfection. The first goal is a calm place to capture what exists and choose the next clear step.

View The Recovery Desk

What usually goes wrong

When life admin piles up, the problem is rarely one task. It is the lack of one trusted place to see everything. Items live in messages, paper piles, notes, email, and memory.

The core system you need

A simple recovery system should capture the task, category, urgency, due date, next action, and status. It should also separate urgent items from items that can wait.

A simple reset structure

  • Capture every loose item.
  • Separate bills, paperwork, appointments, calls, renewals, and personal admin.
  • Mark what is urgent or overdue.
  • Choose the next action for each item.
  • Work from the smallest clear step.
  • Review the system weekly.

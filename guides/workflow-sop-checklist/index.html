How to create a workflow SOP checklist before using AI.

AI works better when the workflow is already clear. A strong SOP checklist turns scattered steps into a repeatable process with ownership, review points, and quality control.

View Workflow SOP & AI Guide

What usually goes wrong

People often try to add AI before the process is ready. The workflow is partly in memory, partly in old notes, and partly in chat. Without a clear SOP, AI has no stable process to support.

The core checklist

  • What starts the workflow?
  • What information is required?
  • Who owns each step?
  • What output is expected?
  • What must be reviewed before completion?
  • What exceptions need human judgment?

How to make it AI-ready

Once the SOP is clear, identify where AI can safely assist: drafting, summarizing, checking, classifying, or preparing work for review. Keep human review where quality, client impact, or judgment matters.

FAQ

What is an SOP checklist?

It is a simple checklist that defines the steps, inputs, owners, outputs, and review criteria for a repeatable workflow.

Why should SOPs come before AI?

Because AI support is safer and more useful when the process has clear rules, context, and review checkpoints.
